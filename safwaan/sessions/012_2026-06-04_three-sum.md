## Key Insight

Sort first. Fix `nums[i]`, then two-pointer the rest of the array looking for a pair summing to `-nums[i]`. Because the array is sorted, duplicates are adjacent — so you skip them in place (`continue` on duplicate `i`; `while` past duplicate `left`/`right` after a match) instead of paying for a Set. O(n²) time, O(1) extra space.
